üöÄ Performance
VrRotation Speed
The speed to pull back on the stick. Must not be less than Vs1 to prevent stall.
VxBest Angle of Climb
Greatest altitude in shortest distance. Use for short fields/obstacles.
VyBest Rate of Climb
Greatest altitude in shortest time. Standard post-takeoff climb speed.
‚ö†Ô∏è Limitations
VaManeuvering Speed
Max speed for full control deflection. Protects structure from breaking before stalling. Decreases as weight decreases.
VfeMax Flaps Extended
Top of the White Arc. Exceeding may damage flaps.
VloGear Operation
Max speed to cycle the gear (Extend/Retract).
VleGear Extended
Max speed to fly with gear already down.
VneNever Exceed
Red Line. Structural failure probable beyond this point.
VnoMax Structural Cruise
Top of Green Arc. Only fly above this in smooth air (Yellow Arc).
VsStall Speed
Bottom of Green Arc. Wing exceeds critical angle of attack.
Vs0Stall (Landing)
Bottom of White Arc. Stall speed in landing configuration (dirty).
üëÆ Regulatory / Multi
V1Decision Speed
Go/No-Go speed. Below V1 = Abort. Above V1 = Continue (even with failure).
V2Takeoff Safety Speed
Target speed to reach by 35ft AGL after engine failure.
VmcMin Control Speed
Red Radial Line. Minimum speed to maintain directional control with critical engine out.
VrefReference Landing
Target speed for final approach (usually 1.3 x Vs0).
VsoStall (Landing Config)
Stalling speed or minimum steady flight speed in the landing configuration.
